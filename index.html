What's inside

Everything your small business needs

Built specifically for sari-sari stores, retailers, and service providers in the Philippines.

🛒

Point of Sale (POS)

Fast checkout with barcode scanning, discounts, split payments, and official receipt generation.

📦

Inventory Management

Track stock levels, get low stock alerts, manage restocks, and monitor expiry dates.

💳

E-Services Tracker

Track GCash, Maya, Eload, Bills Payment, and Padala transactions with fee earnings per service.

📊

Sales Reports

Daily, weekly, and monthly sales summaries with profit margins, COGS, and top-selling products.

👥

Customer & Utang Tracking

Manage customer accounts, track credit balances (utang), and record payments with full history.

💸

Expenses & Draws

Log daily expenses and owner's draws. See net profit after all outflows in the End of Day report.

🌙

End of Day Report

Full closing report with cash drawer reconciliation, variance tracking, and printable summary.

🔒

PIN & Multi-Cashier

Secure your app with a PIN, manage multiple cashier accounts, and track per-cashier performance.

🖨️

Receipt Printing

Print receipts via Bluetooth thermal printer. Customize with your store name, logo, and footer.

📋 Privacy Policy — ByteKit PH

ByteKit PH ("the App") is a sales and inventory tracking application developed for Android devices. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection: ByteKit PH does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely offline. All data you enter — including products, sales, customer records, and transactions — is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

Internet Access: The App does not require internet access to function. No network requests are made during normal use of the App.

Device Storage: The App stores data on your device's local storage. This data is only accessible within the App and is removed when you uninstall the App or choose to reset the data from within the App settings.

Third-Party Services: ByteKit PH does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy: The App is intended for use by adults operating small businesses. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy: Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact: If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at bytekitph@gmail.com
