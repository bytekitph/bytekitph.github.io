What's inside

Everything your small business needs

Built specifically for sari-sari stores, retailers, and service providers in the Philippines.

🛒

Point of Sale (POS)

Fast checkout with barcode scanning, discounts, split payments, and official receipt generation.

Inventory Management

Track stock levels, get low stock alerts, manage restocks, and monitor expiry dates.

E-Services Tracker

Track GCash, Maya, Eload, Bills Payment, and Padala transactions with fee earnings per service.

Sales Reports

Daily, weekly, and monthly sales summaries with profit margins, COGS, and top-selling products.

Customer & Utang Tracking

Manage customer accounts, track credit balances (utang), and record payments with full history.

Expenses & Draws

Log daily expenses and owner's draws. See net profit after all outflows in the End of Day report.

End of Day Report

Full closing report with cash drawer reconciliation, variance tracking, and printable summary.

PIN & Multi-Cashier

Secure your app with a PIN, manage multiple cashier accounts, and track per-cashier performance.

Receipt Printing

Print receipts via Bluetooth thermal printer. Customize with your store name, logo, and footer.

📋 Privacy Policy — ByteKit PH

Effective Date: April 2, 2026  ·  View full policy →

ByteKit PH is a sales and inventory tracking application for Android. This policy explains how your information is handled.

📦 Your Business Data: All data you enter — products, sales, customers, expenses — is stored locally on your device only. It is never sent to ByteKit PH or any third party.

🌐 Internet Access: The App uses internet only for license verification — to activate your access code and confirm it is valid. This communicates only with a private, secure repository. No personal or business data is transmitted. The App works fully offline after activation.

📷 Camera: Used only for barcode scanning and product photos. Never used in the background. No images are uploaded to any server.

📱 Device ID: Your device's anonymous Android ID is used only to bind your license to your device. It is not linked to your name, Google account, or any personal information.

👶 Children's Privacy: The App is intended for adults operating small businesses. It is not directed at children under 13.

Contact: Questions? Email us or message us on Facebook.
